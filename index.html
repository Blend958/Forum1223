The Event

Spker is an invitation only event with a carefully curated audience. You are more than welcome to submit your application.

At a time of exponential changes in digital technology, speed and distraction – leading to dramatic changes in society – the Conference offers profound guidance to you, your team and your company. From 19th to 20th September 2019 we will help shift your perspective on the future and prepare you for the big digital challenges ahead.
